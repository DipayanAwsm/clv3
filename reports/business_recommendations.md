# Business Recommendations: Premium Customer Program

## Objective
Use customer-level CLV segmentation and predictive premium-customer scoring to improve profitable growth, retention quality, and underwriting discipline.

## Recommended Decision Framework
- `Retain & Grow` for high predicted premium-customer probability.
- `Protect` for mid-tier customers with recoverable value potential.
- `Monitor / Re-price` for low-value or loss-heavy profiles.

## Action Playbook by Band
### 1) Retain & Grow
- Prioritize proactive renewal engagement 90-120 days before expiration.
- Offer cross-sell and multi-product bundles to increase stickiness.
- Apply service-tier escalation for high-value households at risk of churn.

### 2) Protect
- Trigger customer-experience interventions for complaints and payment friction.
- Use targeted loyalty offers only where expected value supports investment.
- Monitor trend shifts in claims and loss ratio before renewal.

### 3) Monitor / Re-price
- Route to underwriting review when claim frequency and loss ratio exceed thresholds.
- Tighten pricing, deductible structure, and risk controls for persistent loss makers.
- Introduce payment discipline nudges where delay and delinquency trend upward.

## KPI Framework for Leadership
Track monthly and quarterly outcomes by action band:
- Renewal rate uplift
- Retained earned premium
- Net loss ratio change
- CLV improvement
- Complaint and delinquency trend

## Pilot Recommendation (90 Days)
- Run pilot in 2-3 states/channels.
- Implement score-based treatment in CRM workflow.
- Measure lift versus control cohort.
- Scale based on proven retention/CLV impact and operational readiness.

## Governance and Model Risk Controls
- Refresh scorecards quarterly.
- Monitor drift by geography, product subtype, and channel.
- Validate fairness and stability of threshold-based actions before broad rollout.
